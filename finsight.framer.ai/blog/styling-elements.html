The 50/30/20 Rule vs. Zero-Based Budgeting

Two budgeting styles — one flexible, one precise. Which one fits your financial personality?

🧾 The 50/30/20 Method

  • 50% → Needs (rent, bills, groceries)

  • 30% → Wants (movies, dining out)

  • 20% → Savings & investments


🔢 Zero-Based Budgeting

You assign every rupee a purpose — from your EMI to that coffee subscription. It’s detailed but powerful for those who love control.


⚖️ Choosing What Works

Ask yourself:

  • Do I want ease or precision?

  • Will I actually maintain it long-term?

Remember: the best budget isn’t the perfect one. It’s the one you’ll stick to without burnout.
