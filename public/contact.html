Common Questions

Frequently asked

How quickly can you start a project?

For most engagements we can begin within 1–2 weeks of agreement. Urgent projects can often be started sooner — just mention your timeline in the message.

Do you work with startups as well as enterprises?

Absolutely. We tailor our approach to your scale — from lean MVP builds for early-stage startups to enterprise-grade migrations and support contracts.

What does a typical engagement look like?

We start with a discovery call, follow up with a scoped proposal, then move into delivery with regular check-ins. Most projects run in 2-week sprints with full visibility.

Do you offer fixed-price or time-and-materials contracts?

Both. For well-defined projects we offer fixed-price contracts for peace of mind. For evolving requirements, a time-and-materials model gives maximum flexibility.
